This page currently focuses on two developed products: one is an interactive reflective app, and the other is a pilot-study product for evaluating AI in care-related contexts.

Both projects ask how AI can be designed or assessed without reducing human vulnerability into mere engagement, dependency, or efficiency metrics.

Products

Two concise prototypes: one can be experienced directly, and one introduces a pilot evaluation framework.

相
Interactive App

识相 ShiXiang

A quiet answer book inspired by the Diamond Sutra. Users ask one question; the app responds with one line, one interpretation, and one reflective question.

  • Does not predict the future.
  • Does not make decisions for the user.
  • Designed against addictive companion-product patterns.

Pilot Study

Trauma-Informed AI Care Evaluation

A one-page pilot-study product that maps trauma-informed care principles to corresponding social work ethical principles for AI evaluation in sensitive care contexts.

  • Focuses on safety, power, and self-worth.
  • Turns ethical principles into scorable evaluation language.
  • Supports safer assessment of AI tools for vulnerable communities.
